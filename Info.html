What we do

At the Doherty Robotics Club, students have the option of either working alone or in groups to design and construct a functional robot according to competition criteria and constraints. The competition changes every year as a new tournament is presented, allowing a variety of equipment to be utilized.
All materials are provided by the teacher.
If building and creating robots from just plain sheet metals to a fully operating robot with functionality peeks your interest, then join the club today!
